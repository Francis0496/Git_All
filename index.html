About

A Story of Resilience, Reinvention, and Impact

Francis Sesay's journey is shaped by persistence: growing through the realities of a single-mother upbringing, navigating educational challenges, and learning to turn constraints into creative force.

His path began with Physics with Engineering before a decisive transition into Information Technology, where he became a First Class graduate, Best Graduating Student, and Best Dissertation award recipient.

Today, he builds AI and machine learning systems, leads technical communities, and founded Sierra EduTech to make practical digital learning more accessible to students across Sierra Leone and beyond.
